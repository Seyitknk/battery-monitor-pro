## 🔍 What is battery-monitor-pro?

Battery Monitor Pro is a small, easy-to-use program for Windows. It watches your laptop or tablet battery in real time. This helps you see exactly how much charge you have left and get alerts before the battery runs out. The app also offers automatic features to save power, like switching to power saver mode or putting your machine into hibernation when the battery is low. This way, your device runs longer and lasts healthier over time.

## ⚙️ Key Features

Battery Monitor Pro helps you manage your battery better. It:

- Shows battery charge and status in a clear window or tray icon
- Sends notifications when battery reaches certain levels (e.g., 20%, 10%)
- Automatically switches to power saver mode to reduce energy use
- Hibernates your PC if battery gets too low, preventing data loss
- Logs battery status over time to help you understand battery health
- Runs quietly in the background so you don’t have to keep it open
- Uses a simple interface with easy-to-read alerts suitable for all users

## 🔧 How to Use Battery Monitor Pro

Once the app is running, here’s what you can do:

- **Check Battery Status:** See the current charge as a percentage in the app window or in the system tray icon near the clock.
- **Receive Alerts:** You will get pop-up messages when battery drops to set levels. These warnings help you plug in or save your work.
- **Configure Auto Actions:** Open settings to choose when the app should switch your PC to power saver mode or hibernate automatically.
- **View Battery Logs:** Check past battery performance and how it has changed over time.
- **Customize Notifications:** Adjust which alerts you want or turn them off.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

If you have issues, try these common fixes:

- **App won't open?** Restart your computer and try again.

- **Battery status not updating?** Close the app fully, then reopen it.
- **Automatic actions not working?** Make sure Battery Monitor Pro has permission to change power settings.
